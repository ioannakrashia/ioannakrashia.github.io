Accommodation

Cyprus offers many options for all budgets, but since it is very popular during summer time, make sure you book your accommodation as early as possible!

Larnaca

Budget hotels opposite the venue:

Sveltos Hotel | Crown Resorts Henipa

4-star and 5-star hotels still close to the venue (approx 5 mins taxi ride to the venue):

Sentido Sandy Beach Hotel | Golden Bay Beach Hotel | Lordos Beach Hotel

Paphos

Elysium | Almyra

Ayia Napa & Protaras

This is where you will find some of the best beaches of the island and the lively touristic side of Cyprus. Our favorite hotels are the following, but there are numerous other options covering any budget as well as villas and apartments.

Limassol

Limassol is the town in Cyprus with the nicest restaurants, bars and beach bars but not with the best beaches. It's really worth a visit if you value good food and a good night out.
