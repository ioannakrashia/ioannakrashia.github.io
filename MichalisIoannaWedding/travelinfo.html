Some tips about the main cities in Cyprus

Nicosia

Despite being the capital city and our hometown, it is probably not the ideal destination during summer time as it is really hot and quite far from the beach. We would recommend that you come to Nicosia only for the wedding ceremony. If you are spending more days in Cyprus, you can plan a day trip (or an evening trip when the temperature is lower).

Larnaca

his is where the International Airport is located and where our wedding venue will be. Larnaca is around 40 mins from Nicosia and 30 mins from Ayia Napa and Limassol. A stay here is recommended if you are planning to drink a lot the night of the wedding and want a hotel that is close to the venue, or if your flight leaves the next or the day after the next of the wedding.

Paphos

Paphos is appreciated for its really good hotels and its nature. Akamas forest which is close to Paphos serves for safaris but also boat trips to crystal clear waters (highly recommended!). Should you decide for a trip to Paphos we will provide you with more information.

Ayia Napa & Protaras

This is where you will find some of the best beaches of the island and the lively touristic side of Cyprus. Our favorite hotels are the following, but there are numerous other options covering any budget as well as villas and apartments.

Limassol

Limassol is the town in Cyprus with the nicest restaurants, bars and beach bars but not with the best beaches. It's really worth a visit if you value good food and a good night out.
